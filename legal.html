Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Rachel Whitmore ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website or engage with our services. By using this site, you consent to the practices described here.

If you do not agree with these terms, please discontinue use of the site. We reserve the right to update this policy at any time, and we will indicate the date of the most recent revision at the top of this page.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in the following ways:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact information you voluntarily provide when booking a discovery call, signing up for our email list, submitting a contact form, or purchasing services.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details and billing information processed through secure third-party payment processors. We do not store full payment details on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, and referring sources. This data is collected anonymously and used only to improve your experience.
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device that help us recognize returning visitors and understand site usage. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function as intended.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our coaching services
  • To process transactions and send related confirmations and receipts
  • To send you email communications about our services, resources, and relevant updates — only with your explicit consent, and you may unsubscribe at any time
  • To respond to your inquiries, questions, and support requests in a timely manner
  • To improve and personalize our website, content, and coaching programs
  • To comply with applicable legal obligations and protect our rights

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as necessary to provide our services (e.g., payment processors, scheduling tools) or as required by law.

Rachel Whitmore's coaching services are designed to support your personal growth, spiritual clarity, and emotional wellbeing. However, coaching is not therapy, counseling, psychology, or medical treatment. Rachel Whitmore is not a licensed therapist, psychologist, or mental health counselor.

Coaching does not diagnose, treat, or cure any mental health condition, medical issue, or psychological disorder. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, active trauma symptoms that require clinical intervention, or any medical emergency, please contact a licensed mental health professional or emergency services immediately.

Results from coaching vary based on individual circumstances, readiness, and commitment to the process. We make no guarantees regarding specific outcomes. By engaging in our services, you acknowledge that you are an active participant responsible for your own growth, decisions, and wellbeing.

Refund Policy

We want you to feel confident entering into any coaching engagement. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis and considered within the first 30 days of program enrollment.

If you feel the program is not a fit for you, please reach out directly at hello@rachelwhitmore.com and we will work with you toward a fair resolution. We are committed to your wellbeing and will not leave you stuck in something that isn't serving you.

Discovery calls and free resources carry no cost and therefore no refund applies. Digital products (trainings, courses) may have separate refund terms communicated at the point of purchase.
