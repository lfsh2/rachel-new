Is This Right for You?

This Work Is
For You If…

I want to be genuinely honest with you. This work is specific, and it's not for everyone. Here is how to know if it's the right fit for where you are right now.

You're ready if…
  • You're exhausted from performing and genuinely want something different at the root
  • You're willing to go slowly and do the actual inner work — not just manage symptoms
  • You believe (or want to believe) that God is actually for you, not just tolerating you
  • You're ready to be honest — with yourself, with God, and with a coach who will hold that well
  • You're a Christian woman who wants faith to feel genuinely alive, not just correct
Honest Clarity

This Isn't
For You If…

I'd rather be upfront than have you invest time and energy in the wrong fit. Here's who this work isn't right for right now.

This may not be the right fit if…
  • You're looking for a quick fix or a practical 3-step formula
  • You need clinical mental health treatment — I'll always refer you to the right support
  • You want someone to validate staying exactly where you are
  • You're not willing to examine your beliefs about God, yourself, and what you deserve
  • You want advice-giving and accountability tracking, not transformation-walking

Still not sure? Book a free call. We'll figure it out together — and I'll always be honest if this isn't the right fit, and point you somewhere better if it isn't.
